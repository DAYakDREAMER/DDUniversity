    What about our DORMS!

    At Day Dreamer University, we understand that living on campus is an essential part of the college experience. That's why we offer cozy yet affordable dorm rooms to our students. Our dorms are designed to provide a comfortable living space that feels like home, with modern amenities and a welcoming atmosphere. Our housing options range from single to quad occupancy, with shared community spaces and study rooms to promote socialization and academic success. We believe that college should be accessible to everyone, and our affordable housing options are just one way we strive to make that a reality.     At Day Dreamer University, we believe that finances should never stand in the way of your education. That's why we offer a range of financial assistance programs, including student loan assistance and housing assistance, to help make college more affordable and accessible to everyone. Come explore our financial assistance programs and see how we can help make your college dreams a reality.
